"Akshardham Cricket League" a league or tournament organized by the group of people called "Akshardham Yuvak Mandal" with help and support of few collegian's. The league was first started in the year of 2k16. It is the tournament which is organized in winter and summer of every year. It is the tournament in which only the members living in the society are allowed to play in the match. It is the tournament in which people can participate at very neglegible amount of money. As per the number of players enroll team formation takes place. On average every year approx six teams of eight players are included. Akshardham Cricket League is organized with all the necessary facilites like live recording(to confirm all the decision), Drinking Water, EnergyDrink break, and last but not least ofcourse chairs for the audience as well as for the batting team players. The regular matches are of the EIGHT overs on average and FINAL matches are of TEN on average. At the end of tournament the winning team is awarded with gorgeous trophy and every player of winning team are awarded with certificate. Also the player's who scored maximum runs , shot maximum sixes/fours ,taken maximum wickets etc are also awarded with the medals. The tournament is organized in such a way that whole tournament occurs with the rules and regulations. which makes the tournament really really awesome and enjoyable.Players also who plays enjoys it very much.
